Kyoto, Japan


Kyoto, once the capital of Japan, is a city on the island of Honshu famous for its classical Buddhist temples, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines, and traditional wooden houses. It's a blend of ancient culture and modern charm.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Fushimi Inari Shrine – Famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates.
  2. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove – A serene walk through towering bamboo stalks.
  3. Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) – A stunning Zen temple covered in gold leaf.
  4. Gion District – Traditional area known for geishas and teahouses.
  5. Nijo Castle – A UNESCO World Heritage Site with beautiful gardens.

Must-Try Foods

  • kaiseki – A traditional multi-course Japanese dinner.
  • Matcha (Green Tea) – Especially from the Uji region, known for quality.
  • Yudofu – A tofu hot pot dish, simple yet flavorful.
  • Saba Sushi – A Kyoto-style mackerel sushi specialty.
  • Yatsuhashi – A sweet made from rice flour, sugar, and cinnamon.
